My Philosophy

I’ve had friends ask me what the difference is between Professional Writing and an English major. They cite that the two majors are interchangeable because working with words and writing papers are covered in both. But they are not interchangeable; in fact, they are two entirely different fields.

Professional Writing, and editing more specifically, is similar to math. In a math course, students are tasked with memorizing multiple formulas and using them in various situations to solve unique problems. Editors may get multiple “cheat sheets” in the form of style guides and reference books, but the concept is still the same, editors memorize theories of grammar and work with different articles, papers, resumes, flyers, and what-not to solve just as many more issues.
